Quicksort

arrow keys <- -> to sort (Or A and D)

All you have to do is sort as fast as you can. It sounds easier than it is.

Can you beat everyone else's score and get to the top of the leaderboards? (No signing up required)

Sort by Direction -> Hard is probably the most difficult mode of all of them.
